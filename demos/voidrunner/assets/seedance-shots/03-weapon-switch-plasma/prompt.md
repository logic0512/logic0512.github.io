# 03 Weapon Switch Plasma Caster

## Reference Images

- `start-current-cannon.png`
- `end-plasma-caster.png`

## Goal

The character stays in the customization pose while the right forearm weapon transforms from the default cannon into a compact plasma-caster module.

## Seedance Prompt

Use the first image as the starting frame and the second image as the ending frame. Keep the same character identity and the same pale gray-white studio lighting. The body remains steady in a standing equipment inspection pose. Animate only the right forearm weapon: the default cannon compresses and reconfigures into a shorter, heavier plasma-caster module with a wide barrel, black carbon-metal heat vents, and a circular red energy chamber at the muzzle. Make it feel like a premium game equipment swap: armor panels slide, heat vents open, a red core powers up, faint vapor releases, tiny red highlights pulse. No UI, no text, no logos, no extra characters.

## Settings

- Duration: 2-3 seconds
- Camera: locked
- Output: `weapon-switch-plasma.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not change the character's face, hair, torso, legs, or left claw hand. Do not add a separate gun. Do not make the weapon overly colorful.
