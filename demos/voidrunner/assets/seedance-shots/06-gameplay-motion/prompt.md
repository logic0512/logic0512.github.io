# 06 Gameplay Motion

## Reference Images

- `start-gameplay-hero.png`

## Goal

A short fake 3D gameplay-feel motion clip: the character subtly moves in the rainy cyberpunk street, as if controlled in a third-person game.

## Seedance Prompt

Animate this image as a third-person AAA sci-fi gameplay shot. Keep the same bio-mechanical hunter viewed from behind in the rainy cyberpunk industrial street. The character shifts weight, shoulders roll slightly, exposed back cables sway, the right forearm weapon emits a small red glow, rain and steam move in the environment, wet pavement reflections shimmer, distant pedestrians move subtly, and the camera has a gentle handheld third-person game drift. Keep the character identity stable: white spiky hair, black biomechanical armor, exposed cables, hunched posture, right-arm weapon. No HUD, no UI, no readable text, no logos.

## Settings

- Duration: 3-4 seconds
- Camera: subtle third-person idle movement, no fast pan
- Output: `gameplay-motion.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not turn the character into a different design. Do not add fighting or explosions. Do not add interface elements; webpage HUD will be added later.
