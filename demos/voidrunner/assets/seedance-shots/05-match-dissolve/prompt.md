# 05 Match Dissolve

## Reference Images

- `start-standing-character.png`
- `mid-dissolve-reference.png`
- `end-gameplay-scene.png`

## Goal

The character enters the match: white customization space breaks apart, the character dissolves into dark particles and fog, then appears integrated into the rainy gameplay city scene.

## Seedance Prompt

Use the first image as the starting visual, the second image as the dissolve style reference, and the third image as the ending gameplay environment. Animate the same bio-mechanical humanoid hunter beginning in a pale gray-white studio space, then the UI-free studio fades into mist. The character leans forward as if launching into a match. Edges of the hair, back cables, shoulders, and right forearm weapon break into dark particles and smoky fragments, then the particles are pulled forward into a rainy cyberpunk industrial street at night. End with the character from behind in the gameplay scene, black biomechanical armor visible, white spiky hair readable, red weapon glow small but clear. Make the transition cinematic, premium AAA sci-fi game style, foggy and heavy, not magical. No UI, no readable text, no logos.

## Settings

- Duration: 4 seconds
- Camera: slow forward push through fog
- Output: `match-dissolve.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not fully destroy the character too early. Do not create fire or colorful magic particles. Do not add HUD; HUD will be made later in the webpage.
