# 07 Customization Idle

## Reference Images

- `customization-character-reference.png`

## Goal

Optional equipment-screen idle loop. The character stands in place while the webpage UI handles the actual equipment selection around it.

## Seedance Prompt

Animate this image as a character customization idle loop in a premium AAA sci-fi game. Keep the same standing bio-mechanical hunter in the pale gray-white studio space. The body remains mostly still, with subtle mechanical breathing, small torso weight shift, faint cable sway, tiny servo movements in the shoulders and right forearm cannon, and a soft red light pulse in the optic and muzzle. Keep the full body readable and the camera mostly locked. No UI, no text, no logo, no extra characters.

## Settings

- Duration: 3-4 seconds
- Camera: locked
- Output: `customization-idle.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not switch weapons in this clip. Do not walk, attack, or change the character design.
