# 02 Weapon Switch Rail Lance

## Reference Images

- `start-current-cannon.png`
- `end-rail-lance.png`

## Goal

The character stays in the customization pose while the right forearm weapon transforms from the default cannon into a long rail-lance weapon.

## Seedance Prompt

Use the first image as the starting frame and the second image as the ending frame. Keep the character body, pose, face, hair, armor, cables, legs, left claw hand, lighting, and pale studio background consistent. Animate only the right forearm weapon changing from the bulky default cannon into a long sleek rail-lance integrated into the arm. The transformation should look like a sci-fi equipment swap in a game customization screen: black mechanical plates slide, rails extend forward, small red energy lines ignite along the barrel, micro steam vents, subtle sparks, controlled mechanical calibration. Keep the camera mostly locked and the character steady. No UI, no text, no logos, no extra characters.

## Settings

- Duration: 2-3 seconds
- Camera: locked
- Output: `weapon-switch-rail.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not change the full body pose, do not morph the face or hair, do not add a handheld gun, do not make the weapon detach from the arm.
