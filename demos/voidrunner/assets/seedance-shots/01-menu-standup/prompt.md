# 01 Menu Standup

## Reference Images

- `start-menu-crouch.png`
- `end-standing-customization.png`

## Goal

The character transitions from the crouched main-menu hero pose into a standing customization pose. This is used when the webpage moves from the main menu into the equipment screen.

## Seedance Prompt

Use the first image as the starting frame and the second image as the ending frame. Animate the same bio-mechanical humanoid hunter rising from a low crouch into a standing inspection pose inside a clean pale gray-white studio space. Keep the character identity consistent: pale white-gray spiky synthetic hair, black carbon-metal biomechanical exoskeleton, skull-like mechanical face with one red optic, exposed back cables, oversized right forearm cannon with red glow, long clawed left hand, hunched predator posture. The motion should feel like a premium AAA sci-fi game character selection animation: heavy mechanical joints, subtle cable sway, small red light flicker, slow controlled movement, cinematic product-render lighting. Keep the background minimal and stable, no UI, no text, no logos, no extra characters.

## Settings

- Duration: 3-4 seconds
- Camera: mostly locked, slight cinematic push-in allowed
- Output: `menu-standup.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not change the character design, do not add weapons detached from the arm, do not add text or interface elements, do not make the motion cartoonish.
