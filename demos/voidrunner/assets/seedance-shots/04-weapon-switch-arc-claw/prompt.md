# 04 Weapon Switch Arc Claw

## Reference Images

- `start-current-cannon.png`
- `end-arc-claw.png`

## Goal

The character stays in the customization pose while the right forearm weapon transforms from the default cannon into a melee arc-claw module.

## Seedance Prompt

Use the first image as the starting frame and the second image as the ending frame. Keep the character's identity, body pose, white spiky hair, red optic, black biomechanical armor, exposed cables, and pale studio background consistent. Animate only the right forearm weapon changing from the bulky cannon into a compact arc-claw melee module with three segmented black mechanical talons extending forward from the wrist. The motion should feel like a sci-fi weapon reconfiguration: cannon plates retract, wrist armor rotates, mechanical talons unfold one by one, red energy seams light up between the talons, tiny sparks and calibration pulses. No UI, no text, no logos, no extra characters.

## Settings

- Duration: 2-3 seconds
- Camera: locked
- Output: `weapon-switch-arc-claw.mp4`

## Avoid

Do not change the rest of the body. Do not add fantasy claws, organic flesh, or detached blades. Keep it mechanical and integrated into the forearm.
